ARK Design 招交互设计师

Our group is in search of an art director with 5-7 years experience in creating holistic UI graphics. The right candidate should be a visual storyteller that can plan and define styles for utilities and lifestyle applications. The AD is expected to challenge the brand guidelines and test new grounds for HP.

Job duties include:

  • Art direction lead over a growing, globally distributed visual design team.
  • Create original art.
  • Development of HP visual style for applications, iconography, logotype, and communications collateral.
  • Development of UI transitions and animations to give our products life.
  • Present and convey strategies that justify design decisions.
  • Partnering with UX teams to define tone and character of the applications and services.
  • Partnering with product management teams to improve conversion rates and revenue through visual design.
  • Manage, mentor, and hire designers to support growth.


Qualifications:

  • Original ideas.
  • Strong UI graphics portfolio.
  • Balanced sensibilities for layout and hierarchy.
  • Ability to forecast and incorporate visual trends.
  • 1-2 years leading a creative team.
  • Organized with empathetic time management.
  • Understand UX and able to provide constructive criticism.
  • Fluency in creative and business languages (English and Chinese).
  • Must enjoy complex challenges.
  • Bravery.
